About

The AUCS aims to bring people together over a shared love of cheese,
sharing and growing our knowledge of cheese together.

  • April 2016

    The Founding of an Idea

    The AUCS founding members walk into a Digital Signals tutorial together and note the strange smell. It was soon decided that it was closest to the smell of a certain smoked cheese.

  • May 2016

    Writing a Constitution

    A constitution is strung together and an application is put forward. The Adelaide University Cheese Society is well on its way.

  • August 2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our first event - a cheese toastie sale - is a great success with over a hundred people coming by to taste what this club has to offer.
